Rural Informative & Social Harmony Academy

    Work Done by the Organization:

Who We Are

Rural Informative and Social Harmony Academy is a non-governmental organization established in 1997–98 by Mrs. Risha in Sultanpur, Uttar Pradesh. We work across India with a mission to promote social welfare, education, and community empowerment.

What We Do

At present, we support the care, nutrition, education, and training of 40 mentally challenged children.we work at the grassroots level to ensure that vulnerable populations benefit from government schemes such as pensions and farmer support programs. By promoting social harmony, environmental conservation, and public health awareness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower marginalized communities by promoting education, awareness, and self-reliance. We actively work for the rehabilitation and welfare of persons with disabilities, including providing education, skill training, assistance with UDID cards, disability certificates, medical support, and access to government welfare schemes.
